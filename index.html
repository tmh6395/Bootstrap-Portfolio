About Me

I was born in Bloomington, Minnesota, on 3 June 1995, where I have lived with my mom and grandma for my entire life. We also have three cats and three dogs. The cats are named Paws, Jouk, and Nico. The dogs are a yellow labrador named Boss and two chihuahua siblings named Kiki and Coco.

I graduated from Thomas Jefferson High School in the Spring of 2013 and began taking classes at Normandale Community College in the Fall of the same year. I graduated from NCC in Fall of 2018 and began taking a bootcamp class at the University of Minnesota in January 2019.

I began programming with the intention of creating video games, but my aspirations changed to becoming a full stack web developer. I have a small background in Java and C++ and I am currently learning tools used for web development such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery.
